Campus Life

School Church

Was established in 1978

The Church helps us to maintain organization, teachings, and to create a support system for members. By establishing a church, the Lord ensures that the correct doctrines are taught.



School Library

Was built in 1980

School libraries help students to get authentic information through the books written by reputed scholars who come from different parts of the world. A library plays an important role in creating a school culture, which helps every student to grow on their individual basis as well.



School Auditorium

Was built in 1982

The building was built for plays, concerts, speeches, etc. It has been a key feature for the students and they enjoy it a lot.

Whats new?

  • Attendance

    With new regulations and due to covid we have made a new canteen. It includes a large set of machines that instantly cleans the plated and makes them covid free, the machine also has a self cleaner so it is also clean all the time. We have seats places 2 meters away from each other. Each table a 6 chairs, we make sure that the food is served with distancing so when the students are in a line we make them stand 1.5 meters away from each other. The seats and tables are sanitized from time to time.

  • New school canteen

    Our school uses a very effective way of attendance, each student has a unique id card and at the reception there is a scanner and is the smae place where students enter the school and head to their classrooms. As soon as a student scans the parents or guardians will recive a message and an email conveying that the child has arrived at school. It is also stored on our database.
